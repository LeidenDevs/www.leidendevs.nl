A testing approach that simplifies verification of complex outputs and catches regressions you might otherwise miss.
What to expect:

- Introduction to approval testing concepts
- Practical examples and discussion

### Who should attend

This talk is for anyone working to improve the quality and reliability of the software they build — or support. Whether you're a developer dealing with tricky test cases, refactoring legacy code, in QA, or a product owner looking to better understand the testing process, you'll find practical takeaways here. Curious minds welcome — technical or not.
