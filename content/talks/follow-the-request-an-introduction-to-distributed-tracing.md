Microservices give us flexibility and scale — but they make it surprisingly hard to answer simple questions like "why was that slow?" or "where did that error come from?" This talk walks through distributed tracing from the ground up: what spans and traces are, how they're collected and connected, and how they fit into a modern observability stack. No prior experience needed — just curiosity about how distributed systems tick.
