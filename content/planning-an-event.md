The following document aims to align folks on the considerations when planning an evening.

## Goals

- **Limit total speaker time to around an hour.** This can be split across one or multiple speakers. We run on Thursday evenings, so while we want to share as much as possible, attendees have limited capacity for broadcast-format content after a working day.
- **Leave space between sessions** for conversation, networking, and drinks refills.

## Talk Formats

We support two formats — see the [Speaking page](/speaking/) for full details:

- **Standard talk:** 20–40 minutes, including Q&A.
- **Ignite/Pecha Kucha:** 5 minutes, 20 slides auto-advancing every 15–20 seconds.

An evening typically combines one or two standard talks, optionally preceded or followed by a short Ignite slot.

## Suggested Schedule

| Time  | Slot |
|-------|------|
| 19:00 | Doors open — drinks, informal chat |
| 19:30 | Welcome & housekeeping (5 min) |
| 19:35 | Talk 1 (20–30 min) |
| 19:55 | Break — drinks, chat (15 min) |
| 20:10 | Talk 2 (20–40 min) — *optional* |
| 20:55 | Open discussion / Q&A (10 min) |
| 21:05 | Social — the rest of the evening |

Adjust as needed depending on the number and length of talks.
