## Support Leiden's thriving tech community
LeidenDevs brings together developers, designers, testers, and product thinkers passionate about building great software. Our monthly meetups foster knowledge sharing, networking, and career growth across diverse tech roles and skill levels.

### Why Sponsor Us?

Your sponsorship directly supports our inclusive community by funding venues, refreshments, and speaker expenses. In return, you'll gain visibility among engaged local tech professionals and demonstrate your commitment to the regional developer ecosystem.

### Sponsorship Benefits

* Brand visibility at events and across our digital channels
* Direct access to skilled developers and tech professionals
* Community recognition as a supporter of local tech growth
* Networking opportunities with potential talent and collaborators

Our community values openness, inclusion, and shared growth. We're looking for partners who share these principles and want to invest in Leiden's tech future.

Ready to sponsor? Contact us at [organizers@leiden.dev] to discuss partnership opportunities that align with your goals and our community needs.
